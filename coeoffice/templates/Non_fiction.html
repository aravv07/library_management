Non-Fiction Books

Non-fiction books are packed with factual information, real-life stories, and knowledge to broaden your perspective on the world.

Popular Non-Fiction Genres

  • Biography & Memoir: Real-life stories of individuals, sharing their life experiences and journeys.
  • Self-Help: Books designed to inspire personal growth, mindfulness, and improved well-being.
  • History: Explore past events, movements, and historical figures that shaped our world.
  • Science: Discover the wonders of the universe, biology, physics, and other scientific fields.
  • Politics & Current Affairs: Understanding political systems, global issues, and contemporary society.

Must-Read Non-Fiction Books

  • "Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind" by Yuval Noah Harari

    A captivating exploration of the history of humankind from the Stone Age to the present.

  • "Becoming" by Michelle Obama

    The inspiring memoir of the former First Lady, recounting her life from childhood to the White House.

  • "The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks" by Rebecca Skloot

    The story of Henrietta Lacks and the HeLa cells that changed medical research.

  • "Educated" by Tara Westover

    A memoir about a woman who grows up in a strict and abusive household but escapes to pursue education.
